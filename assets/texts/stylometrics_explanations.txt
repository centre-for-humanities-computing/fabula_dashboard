#### **Word Count**: Number of words in the text
#### **Average Word Length**: Average length of words in the text
#### **Average Sentence Length**: Average number of characters in a sentence in the text
#### **MSTTR**: Type-Token Ratio, computed on a 100 words rolling window. Measure of lexical diversity.
#### **Bzipr**: Compressibility of the text-files as calculated by dividing the original bitsize with the compressed bitsize (using bzip2 compression). Measure of redundance or formulaicity
